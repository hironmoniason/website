The River of Life

Every Journey Tells a Story

Follow the mighty Brahmaputra as it connects communities, cultures, wildlife, and traditions across Assam and North East India.

🇹

Kaziranga

Home to two-thirds of the world's one-horned rhinoceroses. A UNESCO World Heritage Site where wilderness meets community stewardship.

🏝

Majuli Island

The world's largest river island — a living center of Vaishnavite culture, ancient monasteries, and vibrant mask-making traditions.

🌿

Tea Gardens

Sprawling estates that shaped Assam's identity, where community lives intertwine with the rhythm of harvest across every season.

🌳

Manas Reserve

A UNESCO biosphere reserve and tiger sanctuary where community-led conservation is bringing the forest — and its wildlife — back to life.

Environmental Stewardship

Conservation
Through Tourism

Responsible travel contributes to biodiversity protection, environmental awareness, and community-led conservation initiatives across North East India's precious ecosystems.

🦁

Wildlife Protection

Supporting anti-poaching efforts and wildlife corridors that protect tigers, rhinoceroses, elephants, and hundreds of endemic species across the region.

🌊

Wetland Conservation

Preserving the Brahmaputra's floodplain wetlands — critical habitats for migratory birds and rich aquatic biodiversity that sustain millions of lives.

🌳

Forest Stewardship

Community-led reforestation and forest management programs that regenerate degraded lands and protect the biodiversity of North East India's forests.

♿️

Eco Tourism

Low-impact tourism models that channel revenue directly into conservation while minimising the environmental footprint of every journey taken.

Our Story

About Sonar Asom Responsible Tourism Ventures Limited

We work with communities across Assam and North East India to create tourism experiences that generate livelihoods, preserve cultural heritage, and protect natural ecosystems.

🎯

Our Mission

To design and deliver responsible tourism experiences that create meaningful economic opportunities for local communities, preserve Assam's rich cultural traditions, and contribute to the long-term conservation of North East India's extraordinary natural heritage.

🌟

Our Vision

A North East India where tourism serves as a catalyst for community empowerment, cultural pride, and environmental stewardship — where every traveler's journey creates a positive legacy for the people and places they visit.

💎

Core Values
